Connections 2024

Londoners, We Need You!

Connections is a film project that we are working on developing in collaboration with Thrive Ldn and speakers collective. Supported by the Mayor of London.

Are you available on either Saturday 9th December or Sunday 10th December?

Would you like a FREE jumper?

Are you confident, outgoing and passionate about mental health?

We need your help for our next BIG project!

Important Information:

  • We will provide you with the necessary training via Zoom, prior to filming.
  • Volunteers will be divided into four teams.
    • Team 1: Saturday 9th December 9.30-1.30PM
    • Team 2: Saturday 9th December 2.30-5PM
    • Team 3: Sunday 10th December 9.30-1.30PM
    • Team 4: Sunday 10th December 2.20-5PM
  • Our final filming destinations are TBC but you will need to be based around Central London.

If you're interested in volunteering for Connections 2024 please fill out the registration form below and email the completed form to contact@messagesofhope.co.uk

Connections Submission

Submit a video to be featured in our Video, Connections.

Here’s how it works:

  • We are asking Londoners to submit a video of themselves holding up a message of hope they have written themselves.
  • Your video will then be featured in our video, Connections, due to be released on Great Mental Health Day 2024.

We are encouraging you to be as creative as possible!

Draw posters, banners and get creative! Once you have finished, take a video of yourself holding your message and submit your video below.

Gather your friends, family, co-workers, or classmates! Feel free to submit a group video if you prefer.

If you’re stuck for time and ideas, you can simply write your message on an A4 piece of paper or even a post it note!

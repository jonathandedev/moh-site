World Mental
Health Day

On the 10th October 2022, Messages of Hope was invited to be a part of Thrive LDNs World Mental Health Day event called Never Alone LDN in City Hall, London.

Our aim for this event was to create a mural that displayed anonymous messages of hope that could be showcased at City Hall, and that's exactly what we did. We were able to collect over 200 messages for this event and approximately 50 messages from attendees of the event, on the day.

Following the event we continue to share the messages that were collected for this project. Mental Health Awareness Week 2023 saw the beginning of an exciting collaboration between Thrive LDN's Getting Through This Together campaign and Messages of Hope.

Our joint aim is to work together to bring everyones messages to life by sharing them publicly though Instagram.

We aim to encourage the conversation around mental health and help to break the stigma that surrounds it. As well as highlighting professional and urgent support available for anyone that may need it.

You can support the Messages of Hope project by following @throughtogether or @messagesof.hope on Instagram.
